About

me in 10 seconds

As a Salesforce architect based in Detroit, MI, I’m passionate about the synergy between people and technology, continually exploring how software can transform everyday experiences.



Currently, I’m at SiriusXM, where I enhance the audio experience for our customers—merging my love for technology with my deep appreciation for music. Before joining SiriusXM, I led Salesforce development at Builders Vision, an innovative venture, foundation, and asset management firm dedicated to creating a healthier, more humane planet. This role allowed me to fuse purpose with technology, delivering solutions that drive meaningful impact.



I thrive on building from the ground up, with a focus on Salesforce development and implementations. To me, digital products are living entities—constantly evolving and adapting, much like the world around us.
