About

me in 30 seconds

I'm a Salesforce architect based in Detroit, MI, passionate about the synergy between people and technology. I'm continually exploring how software can transform everyday experiences and make an impact.



Currently, I work at SiriusXM, enhancing the audio experience for customers by merging my love for technology with my deep appreciation for music. Previously, I led Salesforce development at Builders Vision, an innovative venture, foundation, and asset management firm focused on creating a healthier, more humane planet. This role allowed me to combine purpose with technology, delivering solutions that drive meaningful change.



I thrive on building solutions from the ground up, specializing in Salesforce development and implementations. To me, digital products are like living entities—constantly evolving, adapting, and growing alongside the world around us.

A little more about me

Passion:

I'm dedicated to transforming organizations through technology, helping them streamline operations and amplify their impact on communities worldwide.

Background:

My journey from Salesforce user to architect has given me a unique perspective on solution design. I combine hands-on development experience with collaborative problem-solving to create systems that truly serve user needs.

Hobbies:

Away from the keyboard, I balance technology with creativity and exploration. You might find me immersed in a good book, discovering new restaurants by bike, or capturing street photography around Detroit.

Personality:

I approach challenges with genuine curiosity and embrace feedback as fuel for growth. My optimistic outlook helps me turn complex problems into opportunities for innovation in both my work and personal life.
